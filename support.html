Frequently Asked Questions

How do I install ThinkPhish?
Visit the Chrome Web Store, click “Add to Chrome,” and follow the on-screen instructions to get started.

What if ThinkPhish isn’t working as expected?
Please ensure you have the latest version installed. Try refreshing your Gmail page, and if the problem persists, please reach out.

How do I share feedback or request a new feature?
We love hearing from our users! Feel free to submit any suggestions or report through our mail thinkphishofficial@gmail.com .

Does ThinkPhish store any of my personal data?
No. ThinkPhish runs entirely on your browser and does not collect or store any personal or email data.

Is ThinkPhish compatible with other Chrome extensions?
Yes, in most cases. However, some security or mail extensions may interfere with its functionality. Try disabling other extensions one by one if you face conflicts.

How often is ThinkPhish updated?
We frequently push updates to improve accuracy, performance, and security. Chrome will auto-update it, or you can manually check via the Extensions settings.

Can I use ThinkPhish on mobile devices?
Currently, ThinkPhish is designed for desktop Chrome browsers only. Mobile support is under consideration for future releases.
