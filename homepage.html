About the Creator

Hello, I'm Manoj Varshan, a BTech Cyber Security student with a deep passion for innovation, and online safety. I firmly believe that coding isn’t just about prior experience—it’s about creativity, relentless curiosity, and mastering the right tools.

As an experienced AI tools user and a prompt engineering enthusiast, I created ThinkPhish not just as a project—but as a mission. This Chrome extension was developed entirely using AI-assisted development and prompt-driven design, blending technical skill with real-world need.

ThinkPhish uses advanced heuristics, redirect path analysis, and a finely tuned SVM (Support Vector Machine) model to intelligently detect and block phishing attempts in your Gmail inbox. It’s designed to act as a real-time guardian—keeping your digital identity and sensitive information secure from deceptive attackers.

This extension is built with care, precision, and a clear goal: to make cybersecurity accessible and proactive for everyday users. I genuinely hope this helps make your inbox a safer place.
